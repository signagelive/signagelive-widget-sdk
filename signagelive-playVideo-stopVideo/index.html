Signagelive.playVideo() / Signagelive.stopVideo()

playVideo: Used to play video natively on Samsung SSP Tizen displays. Native playback can offer a better visual experience over standard HTML5 video when used in a playlist with other videos and when wanted to render 2 videos with video concurrently.

stopVideo: Used to stop a video that is currently playing as a result of a call to Signagelive.playVideo(). This also removes it from the DOM.
